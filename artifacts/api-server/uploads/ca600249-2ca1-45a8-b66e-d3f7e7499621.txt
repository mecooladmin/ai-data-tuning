Meeting Minutes - Board of Directors
January 15, 2024

Attendees: John Smith (CEO), Sarah Johnson (CFO), Michael Brown (CTO)

The meeting was called to order at 9:00 AM by Chairman John Smith.

On March 3, 2023, the company signed a major partnership agreement with TechCorp Inc., valued at $5 million. The agreement was approved by the board on February 28, 2023.

Sarah Johnson announced on January 10, 2024 that Q4 revenues exceeded projections by 23%, reaching $12.4 million.

Michael Brown reported that the engineering team launched the new cloud platform on December 1, 2023 after 6 months of development.

The board voted to approve the 2024 budget of $15 million. The vote was unanimous.

On April 5, 2023, the company filed a patent application for its proprietary AI algorithm with the US Patent Office. The application is pending review.

Dr. Emily Chen was appointed as Chief Science Officer on November 15, 2023, effective January 1, 2024.

The company established its Singapore office on June 20, 2023 to expand operations in Southeast Asia.

Meeting adjourned at 11:30 AM.
